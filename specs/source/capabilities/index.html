Overview of Capabilities

Capabilities are functionalities necessary to implement credentials on the Web. We include capabilities specific to credentials but also capabilities that support a healthy credentials ecosystem, such as security, as it is important to understand the full context in which credential issuance, storage, and consumption occur.

We organize capabilities into four groups.

  • Issuing
  • Storage
  • Management
  • Consumption
